Why Us

Welcome to ILEC

Welcome to the International Language and Education Center (ILEC). Quality Learning, More Opportunities.
ILEC is a private professional language centre based in Yaounde created in April 2014, authorized by MINEFOP and is in partnership with high standing universities around the world. We have been operating for a very long time now, our tutors worked with the British Council so they have years of teaching experience and the office is very accessible located at the heart of town. We teach English and German language, Prepare students for UK diploma qualifications, Offer exam registration and preparatory classes for IELTS, TOEFL, GRE, GMAT, SAT, Zertifikat Deutsch. We also facilitate student fee payments and offer translation services, Canadian immigration, Uk university pathway and study abroad services for UK, USA and Canada.
